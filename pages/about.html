Our Story

The Interastral Peace Corporation (IPC) is a multi-credillion corporation that is aimed at doing one thing: establishing Peace across the universe. Our founders, the Aeon of Preservation Qlipoth, Louis Fleming, and Dongfang Qixing, are responsible for establishing the economy throughout the cosmos as we know it today. Just about anywhere you'll find IPC-mandated tech, IPC carriers fulfill trades billions of times per day, and many planets are close partners and clients working with the IPC. As you might imagine, the functionality of the IPC is incredibly important to the planets and people of the vast cosmos. You too could contribute to this.
