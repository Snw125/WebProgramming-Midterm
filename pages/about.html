  • Spread our caretaking knowledge to every corner of the universe

Our Story

Mystic Menagerie was founded in 1999 by renowned veterinarian Yao Shi, who noted that mystical creatures were often improperly cared for and misunderstood by the general public. Mystic Menagerie was meant to be a hub of knowledge about these gentle creatures, and a place where we could learn to integrate them with human society. We hope that visiting our site makes you eager to learn more about these silly little guys.


Our Impact

Over 5,000 creatures have been adopted from Mystic Menagerie!
